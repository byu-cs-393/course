# Scheduling Time with the Instructor

## Step 1 — Fix your time zone (do this first!)

Appointment times are shown in *your* Microsoft time zone. If it's wrong, you'll book the
wrong time. Set it to **Mountain Time** so it matches campus:

1. Go to [myaccount.microsoft.com](https://myaccount.microsoft.com) and sign in with your
   **BYU credentials**.
2. Open **Settings & Privacy → Language & region** (or the time-zone setting).
3. Set your time zone to **(UTC−07:00) Mountain Time (US & Canada)** and save.

## Step 2 — Book a time

Book a slot on the instructor's Microsoft Bookings page:
**[tinyurl.com/mtr26](https://tinyurl.com/mtr26)**

Pick an open slot and book it. If you anticipate needing more time, book multiple contiguous
slots.
