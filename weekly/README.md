# Weekly Study (Practice)

Study is the **Practice** part of your grade. Each week you submit a **Study assignment** in
Canvas logging your hours and problems. Target **~9 hours/week** (class counts as 3).

> These templates are the source for the Canvas Study assignments.

## Rubric (13 pts/week)

| Points | For |
|---:|---|
| 4 | **Collaborative study** — 4 hrs (class counts toward this) |
| 4 | **Required problems** done (2 assigned HW + in-class problems) |
| 5 | **Personal study** — 5 hrs (may be collaborative, but doesn't have to be) |

Roughly **half your time on your own, half with others**. On **OA weeks**, the OA replaces
the 2 assigned HW — the in-class problems are your required ones.

> **How to paste a submission URL:** link to your *accepted* submission, not the problem page.
> On LeetCode: open the problem → **Submissions** tab → click your accepted attempt → copy the
> address bar — e.g. `https://leetcode.com/problems/two-sum/submissions/1046917577/`.

## Template — early weeks (richer)

```
- Required problems (paste your accepted-submission URLs):
    1.
    2.
- In-class problems (URLs or "done in class"):
- Collaborative study: __ hrs (with whom?)
- Personal study: __ hrs
- What did you do to make a problem more valuable to your growth?
    [ ] Re-did it and timed myself
    [ ] Re-did it without looking up syntax/solutions
    [ ] Studied others' solutions to learn from them
    [ ] Just finished and moved on
    [ ] Other:
- Would you like a TA/instructor to review a solution and give feedback? (Yes / No)
```

