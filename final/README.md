# Final Exam

**You must pass the final exam to pass the course.**

The target is to solve any medium-level question with roughly a **40%–60% acceptance rate**.

## Format

- **12 questions** over **5½ hours**.
- Solve **3 to pass** (60%); solve **5 for 100%**.
- **Partial credit:** passing half the test cases counts as a partial on up to 2 problems.
  Two partials = one solved (for up to two partials).
- Held on the **last two days of class**, in the same room.

## Hints

Yes — you may ask for hints, like a real interview. Hints help you get unstuck and move to
a partial, or from a partial to solved. We aim to help you with about one problem per day of
the exam.
